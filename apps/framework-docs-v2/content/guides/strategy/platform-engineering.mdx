# Platform Engineering

This guide covers how to implement platform engineering practices to build and maintain self-service data infrastructure.

## Overview

Platform engineering focuses on building internal developer platforms that enable teams to work with data infrastructure efficiently and independently.

## Key Concepts

- Self-service infrastructure
- Developer experience optimization
- Internal platform design
- Automation and tooling
- Platform team organization
